About Us

Alfido Tech is a privately held IT services and consulting company headquartered in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. Founded in 2023, the company has rapidly grown to a team of over 50 professionals, offering a diverse range of digital solutions including custom software development, web and mobile app design, SEO optimization, social media marketing, and Google Ads management.

A notable aspect of Alfido Tech is its commitment to nurturing tech talent through its AICTE-approved remote internship programs. These programs provide hands-on experience in various domains such as frontend development, Python, Java, data science, UX/UI design, and Android development, catering to students and fresh graduates. The internships are structured to offer real-world projects, mentorship from industry experts, and professional certifications upon completion.

Under the leadership of CEO and founder Bhanu Pratap Biswas, Alfido Tech has been recognized for its innovation and growth, earning a spot among HackerNoon's Top 10 Startups in Hyderabad for 2024. The company's dedication to empowering businesses and individuals through technology positions it as a dynamic player in the IT industry.
